IMPORTANT TO NOTE FOR THOSE WHO ARE ON HEMODIALYSIS

IMPORTANT TO NOTE FOR THOSE WHO ARE ON HEMODIALYSIS: (1) There are few Mistakes which people do while on Dialysis, They start eating what they like, Consumes too much fluid or water and overburdens failing Kidneys. Always Monitor Weight between Inter-dialytic period.Try not to exceed weight by more than 3 kg between two consecutive dialysis.One must monitor weight before and after dialysis.
(2) Generally, serious conditions are caused due to imbalances of Biochemical nutrients, so these parameters must be observed regularly.Potassium is most serious to watch, must not be allowed to exceeds by 7.5 as it may become a cause for heart attack. Less or More Sodium and Phosphorus is also not desirable. It can be maintained by taking caution on what you are eating.

(3) Monitoring of Blood Pressure during, before and after Dialysis is also very important.In case Dialysis technician or center not doing it, they must be asked to do so. Blood Pressure is well known silent killer.
(4) At a regular interval, Repoetin and Iron sucrose must be injected to maintain Hemoglobin level.
(5) As Dialysis removes important nutrients from blood during Dialysis, It is necessary to take supplements and nutrients and Proteins orally after every dialysis to keep the body in better condition .
