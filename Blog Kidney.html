Know about Kidney Failures
There are mainly two types of disaeses seen in Kidneys
(1) Acute Renal Failure _ Occurs suddenly and chances of recorvering are very very high and recovers fast.
(2) Chronic Kidney Disease - Occurs over a period of time , progressive in nature and chances of recovery very very low. Although one can delays and manage with it after taking proper medications and Diets. Both above types can be diagnosed easily by simple blood test as Kidney Function Test / KFT where creatinine and urea in serum are clear indicative of stage. After certain deterioration dialysis in only solution which is filtering blood through machine.
